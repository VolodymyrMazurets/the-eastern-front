    Who we are

    The Eastern Front was founded 2016 with a vision to build 1/35 scale diorama pieces to be enjoyed by the general public. For those who are not familiar, dioramas are framed, three-dimensional art pieces presenting a part of military history, in our case mainly WWII. Every diorama concept first starts off by the building of plastic or resin model kits, molded by some of the worldâ€™s most famous kit makers. Modelers with some experience will remember names like Verlinden, Tamiya and Dragon. After the kits are built, the vehicles and soldiers go to the painting stage, where acrylics, enamels and oils are used in a step by step process to gain maximum realism. The final stage is arrangement of the pieces on a frame to depict various dramatic elements of the war. Finished products provide hours of viewing enjoyment, whether you are an experienced modeler or just a fan.
